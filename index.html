What is CherryUX?

CherryUX works with marketing and development teams to provide user experience solutions and experimentation. We build and run A/B tests and analyse the data to verify improved conversions.

What is our workflow?

Each experiment begins in a ideation phase and on confirmation we design and build the code to sit on top the existing application. After passing rigorous quality assurance (QA) testing we run the 'variations' against a 'control' and monitor the results.

What are the risks?

The possibility of an experiment failing is not uncommon, however due to the nature of how split testing works, the percentage of allocated users can be reduced or an experiment 'turned 'off' to mitigate losses.

What are the benefits?

A 'winning' experiment increasing conversions by 5% or more, can be immediately deployed. Your business will instantly benefit from the increased conversion rate. The turnaround time of experiments is miniscule.
